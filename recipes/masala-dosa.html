Masala Dosa

Description

Masala Dosa is a popular South Indian dish consisting of a crispy crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter. It is typically filled with a spiced potato mixture and served with coconut chutney and sambar, offering a delightful combination of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (skinned black gram)
  • 1/4 cup chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Potatoes, onions, and spices for the masala filling

Steps

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, chana dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 6 hours or overnight.
  2. Grind the soaked ingredients to form a smooth batter. Ferment the batter for 8-12 hours.
  3. Cook the potato masala with onions and spices for the filling.
  4. Spread a ladleful of batter on a hot griddle, add the potato masala, and cook until crisp.
  5. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
